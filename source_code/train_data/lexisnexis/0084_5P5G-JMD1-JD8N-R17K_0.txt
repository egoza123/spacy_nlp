Secure Energy Services Inc., an energy services company, completed the acquisition of common shares of Ceiba Energy Services Inc., a service provider of stand-alone water disposal and oil treating facilities to the oil and gas industry, for a purchase consideration of approximately C$37 million (MM) (US$26.97 MM), including the assumption of C$11 MM (US$8.02 MM) of debt.
