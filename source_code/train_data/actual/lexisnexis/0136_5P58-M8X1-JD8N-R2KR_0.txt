ARM Holdings plc, a semiconductor intellectual property (IP) company that designs and develops processors and related technologies, has acquired Simulity Labs Limited, a software company focused in the development of embedded communications software and related server-based applications, from Foresight VCT plc for approximately GBP11.7 million (US$15.06 million).
