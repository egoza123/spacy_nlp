MALAYSIAN DAILY STOCK REPORT Corporate Wire Date: 04 August 2017 01:55 MYT ORIGINAL ANNOUNCEMENT [Text] Manulife Holdings Berhad ("the Company") - Cessation of Equity Interest In Globetronics Technology Berhad by Manulife Asset Management Services Berhad, a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Company Pursuant to paragraph 9.19(26) of Bursa Malaysia Securities Berhad Main Market Listing Requirements, the Board of Directors of the Company wishes to announce that its wholly-owned subsidiary, Manulife Asset Management Services Berhad (Company No. 834424-U) had on 31 July 2017 ceased to be a substantial shareholder of Globetronics Technology Berhad (Company No. 410285-W) further to the disposal of shares in connection with its ordinary business in fund management.
